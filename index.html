Bito's Natcats Rarity

Natcats is the first art collection created from Digital Matter. Each Natcat has unique traits, and is generated autonomously through the Bitcoin blockchain. Visit official website.

I've used the Rarity Score Model. A similar formula is used by the best-performing tools for Ethereum-based NFT collections. It excels at striking a balance between highlighting a single rare trait and appreciating multiple less rare traits simultaneously.

How exactly is it calculated?

Step 1 - Determine the rarity score of each trait
Formula - Rarity score for trait=1/%value of rarity in the collection
Example: Rarity score for laser eyes right = 1/%2.5 = 0.4

Step 2 - Sum up all trait scores
